App Review Notes for Apple / Google:

1. SUBSCRIPTION INFORMATION
   - This app offers auto-renewable subscriptions
   - Plans: Família (Family) and Central
   - Subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat
   - Payment is processed through the App Store / Google Play
   - Users can manage/cancel subscriptions through their device settings
   - Free tier available with no payment required

2. BACKGROUND LOCATION
   - Used for geofencing (safe zone monitoring for elderly users)
   - Alerts family when elderly user leaves predefined safe zones
   - Required for emergency SOS location sharing
   - Permission requested with clear purpose explanation

3. HEALTH/SENSOR DATA
   - Accelerometer used for fall detection only
   - No data is sent to external servers without explicit consent
   - All health data stored locally on device
   - LGPD compliant (Brazilian data protection law)

4. NOTIFICATIONS
   - Critical for check-in system functionality
   - Used for medication reminders
   - Emergency SOS alerts
   - Fall detection alerts

5. TEST ACCOUNT
   - Create account during onboarding (no external auth required)
   - Select "Sou o Idoso" (I'm the elder) or "Sou Familiar" (I'm family)
   - All features testable in free tier
   - Subscription paywall accessible from Settings

6. EMERGENCY SERVICES
   - App provides direct dial to Brazilian emergency services (SAMU 192, Fire 193, Police 190)
   - SOS requires 3-second hold to prevent accidental activation
   - App does not replace professional emergency services
