Estou Bem — Smart care for the ones you love

Estou Bem (I'm OK) is a daily check-in app that connects families and protects elderly loved ones. Simple for the user, complete for the caregiver.

HOW IT WORKS
• The elderly person receives an alarm and taps a button to confirm they're OK
• If they don't respond, family members are notified automatically
• Sensors detect movement and falls to reduce false alarms
• In emergencies, a 24/7 human response center can be activated

FEATURES
✅ One-tap daily check-in — large, accessible interface
✅ Medication management with stock tracking and refill alerts
✅ SOS button with direct calls to family and emergency services
✅ Fall detection via phone accelerometer
✅ GPS and geofencing — alerts when the elderly leaves safe zones
✅ Complete family dashboard with history and statistics
✅ Health diary (blood pressure, glucose, weight, temperature)
✅ Apple Watch and wearable integration

PLANS
• Free: 1 check-in/day, 3 contacts, SOS button
• Family ($9.99/mo): Multiple check-ins, medications, sensors, GPS
• Central ($17.99/mo): Everything + 24/7 human response center, telemedicine

WHY ESTOU BEM?
→ Peace of mind at a fraction of the cost of a caregiver
→ Interface designed for elderly users: large fonts, minimal steps
→ No biometric data blocks the alarm — all data informs who will act
→ Full privacy: encrypted data, LGPD/GDPR compliant

For families who can't be there all the time but want to care. Estou Bem: care that connects.
